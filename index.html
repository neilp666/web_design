Once upon a time there was a group of shapes. They were mostly black and rectangular. They were mean and very serious

And so they kicked the 3 shapes that didn't conform out of their gang , which was called the Right Angled Gang.

And so these three shapes were very sad, but found great joy and comfort being around each other. They soon started having a lot of fun, started rocking and rolling , started being silly , and became the most well-know shape gang in the universe.

They even started making fun of squares and rectangles in what they like to call 'Square Offs'!
